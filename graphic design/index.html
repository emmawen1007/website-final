PROJECT 6: Influencing Campaign, Selling an Idea This project will utilize all of the design and software skills that you have gained in this class. After having viewed the examples in the lecture you can see that there are many ways to approach the creation of an influencing design. Most importantly, you are trying to make people aware of a point of view and/or persuade them of your view. Think about an issue or stance that you think has been misrepresented in society today. How can you correct this view? The content of this design project will be entirely up to you. You should as always consider who your potential audience is and be especially clear about the message you are trying to convey. Directions: • Decide on your message **the text should be your own** • You should use only drawn elements, no photos. • Create at least 2 full-size color sketches • You will create your design to take TWO different forms: o One should be more complex with image and text, the other can be smaller Examples: Poster and button Tote bag and social media post T-shirt and hat Billboard and bumper sticker
